# Healthspan system card

- Status: working access-controlled prototype
- Period: July 2026
- Ownership: end-to-end product and systems design
- Surfaces: iPhone, watchOS, API, worker, and web dashboard
- Source: private; walkthrough available to hiring teams

## Core decision

The product does not claim a universal recovery score. Derived results expose
inputs, provenance, coverage, baseline, confidence, algorithm version,
limitations, and reassessment period.

## Claim boundary

General-wellness prototype, not a medical device or production clinical system.
